### Coverage Notes

- **Early years (1990–1995)**: Concert posters were uncommon in the club era; very few documented
- **Peak coverage (2008–2016)**: 83–94% coverage — the Ten Club poster program was well-established
- **Gap (2018–2021)**: TCB archive ends Jul 2018; Shopify store starts ~2020; COVID cancelled 2020 tour
- **2022+**: Shopify body_html for 2022 posters lists "REGULAR SHOW EDITION" with no artist name
